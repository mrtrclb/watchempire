2. **İlk Modelini Tasarla** — Ar-Ge ekranında kasa, kadran, komplikasyon ve üretim tercihlerini belirle.
3. **Üretim Planını Ver** — Üretim adedi ve kâr marjını seç.
4. **Günü İlerlet, Sonuçları Oku** — Satış, nakit, stok ve itibar değerlerin değişir.
5. **Büyü ve Sezonu Kapat** — Sertifika al, yeni kategorileri aç, sıralamada yüksel.

## 🇬🇧 English

> Build your own watch brand, design models, and outcompete your rivals. Rule the market.

### How to Play

1. **Build Your Brand** — Choose your brand name, logo, and production location. These early decisions directly affect your cost, quality, and capacity.
2. **Design Your First Model** — In the R&D screen, select case, dial, complications, and production preferences.
3. **Set Your Production Plan** — Choose production quantity and profit margin.
4. **Advance Days, Read Results** — Sales, cash, inventory, and reputation change each day.
5. **Grow and Close the Season** — Earn certifications, unlock new categories, climb the rankings.

### Features

- 🏭 8 watch categories — Economy to Super Luxury
- ⚙️ R&D system — 20+ production parameters, 18 complications
- 👥 Staff system — 6 roles, salary and inflation mechanics
- 📜 8 certifications — COSC, Swiss Made, ISO 9001 and more
- 🏆 100 AI rivals, real-time market rankings
- 🤝 Partnerships & Offers — 5 random offers per season
- 🕰️ Vintage Market — 36 fictional watches, trend-based pricing, cross-season portfolio
- 🏦 Bank Credit — reputation-based limit, daily interest, manual repayment
- 👥 Friend League — create a league code, invite friends, compete together
- ☁️ Cloud Save — load your game across browsers with WE-XXXXXX code
- 💾 Offline PWA — installable, no internet required
- 🌐 Full Turkish / English support
- 🏅 51 achievements
- ⚠️ Production line disruption system
- 🏁 Milestone system
- 🗣️ Dynamic Review System — 30 unique reviewers with different perspectives (design, technical, value). A pool of 1200+ comments reacts to your production decisions.  
- 📰 Random Events — Events appear every 2 in-game days, affecting both you and your competitors with gains, losses, and unexpected opportunities.

### Tech Stack

Vanilla JavaScript · Single-file HTML · Firebase RTDB · GitHub Pages

> No frameworks or libraries. Runs as a single HTML file, installable as an offline PWA.

### Privacy

All names in the game are fictional and do not represent any real brand, person, or organization. Runs entirely in your browser, no personal data is collected. Completely free.
